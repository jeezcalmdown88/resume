About Me

That's me after a full day of work helping create Echelon 2020, our ECE Dept. Fest in SMIT.

Welcome to my (informal) Resumé. You might think this looks ugly, but I really like it this way. Reasons:

  1. Loads very fast.
  2. Is super responsive on every type of device.
  3. Looks the same everywhere.
  4. And goes straight to the content.

The only real problem here is that you might think that I am only capable of making websites as such. But that is far from the truth. Yes, I admit that right now I can only make websites of this kind... but one day, I will make great websites. I am a diligent person, so I will learn more. But right now, this is the only web development I can do. Perhaps I wll use a "superior" framework like React or Vue or something in the future. Never on this site, though. This is my personal space on the web. I would never make this site go through a cookie cutter template. I want to embibe the philosophy of less is more through this website.

All day, every day, our brains are bombarded with fast-paced videos, gifs, images, music and what not. I want this website to be a... peaceful garden, rather. A place where you can get away from all that noise and focus on what is important. And the important thing here is me. My skillset. Well, thank you for visiting!
